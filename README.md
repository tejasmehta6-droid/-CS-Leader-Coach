# CS Leader Coach Skill

A Claude skill that acts as a senior Customer Success leadership coach and mentor — built for the AI-transformed SaaS landscape.

## What This Skill Does

This skill coaches CS leaders at every level (CSM → Director → VP → CCO) through any CS business situation with a three-part coaching approach:

1. **Diagnoses before advising** — asks sharp clarifying questions to understand the real situation
2. **Challenges flawed framing** — directly calls out when a leader is asking the wrong question
3. **Delivers usable outputs** — scripts, email templates, and talking points ready to use immediately

## Coverage Areas

| Domain | What's Covered |
|--------|---------------|
| **Revenue & NRR** | Churn saves, at-risk playbooks, expansion motion, renewal strategy, board NRR narratives |
| **People Management** | AI-era hiring, performance management, PIPs, burnout, team culture, 1:1 frameworks |
| **AI Integration** | Tactical tool adoption + strategic CS team redesign for the AI era |
| **Executive Stakeholders** | Board narratives, CS-Sales alignment, EBR design, difficult customer conversations |
| **CS Ops & Tooling** | Health score design, tech stack decisions, playbook frameworks, dashboards |
| **Career Strategy** | Director → VP → CCO positioning, interview coaching, personal brand, negotiation |

## How to Install

1. Download this repository or the `.skill` file
2. Upload to your Claude skills directory
3. The skill will trigger automatically when you discuss CS leadership situations

## Trigger Examples

This skill activates when you say things like:
- *"My CSM is struggling with an at-risk account"*
- *"How do I talk to the board about our NRR?"*
- *"Should I put someone on a PIP?"*
- *"How do I integrate AI into my CS team?"*
- *"I have a $2M renewal at risk"*
- *"How do I position myself for a VP CS role?"*
- *"My team is burned out"*
- *"How should I structure my EBR?"*

## Design Philosophy

- **Diagnose first, advise second** — never gives advice without understanding the situation
- **Challenge the frame** — calls out when a leader is approaching a problem the wrong way
- **Commercial always** — connects every situation back to NRR, retention, and growth
- **AI-forward** — proactively addresses how AI changes the answer in every domain
- **Human advisory tone** — framework backbone with a real senior advisor voice

## Built For

- Customer Success Managers growing into leadership
- CS Managers and Team Leads building their first teams
- Directors of CS building toward VP roles
- VP CS leaders owning NRR and presenting to boards
- CCOs and CS executives navigating AI transformation
